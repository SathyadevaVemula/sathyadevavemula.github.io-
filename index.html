About

I'm a data engineer and analyst with 4+ years of experience building production-grade pipelines that enterprises stake decisions on. My specialty is turning messy, siloed, unreliable data into systems that perform medallion architecture on Azure Databricks, governed platforms with Unity Catalog, and Snowflake warehouses at scale.

At Morningstar, I identified $200K+ in annual cloud cost savings through systematic SQL profiling and warehouse right-sizing while processing 40M+ daily records at 99%+ reliability. At US Foods, I cut pipeline run times by 35% by resolving data skew that had plagued the platform for years.

I care deeply about the full stack of data quality schema enforcement at ingestion, dbt transformation standards at the logic layer, row-level security at reporting, and lineage visibility across all of it. Bad data is a business problem, and I treat it that way.

Currently in Columbus, OH — open to relocation and onsite or hybrid roles where infrastructure quality, platform governance, and business impact are taken seriously.
